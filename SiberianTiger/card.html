Siberian Tiger

Siberian tigers are an endangered species, but there is a specialized group in Russia that is attempting to bring their numbers up.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera tigris altaica
  • Average Length: 11 feet
  • Average Lifespan: 15-20 years
  • Habitat: Coniferous birch woodlands of eastern Russia

Siberian tigers are the largest of any cat in the world. They can be upward of 11 feet and easily weigh over 700 pounds.
